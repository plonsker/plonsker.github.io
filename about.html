Joe Plonsker | About

I am an Emergency Medical Technician currently working and living in my hometown of San Francisco, CA. I attended St. John's College in Santa Fe, NM, where I earned my BA in Liberal Arts (Philosophy and Mathematics Double Major). I paused my decade-long career in software and tech last year in order to pursue the things that matter most to me: working as a first responder, teaching the fundamentals of software engineering to students at local high schools, and, most importantly, raising a young family.

I am probably most well-known for my street photography, which I have stopped sharing online for the time being because it generally looks better in print. Send me an email and perhaps I can send you a zine.
