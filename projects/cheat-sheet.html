This study sheet is based on how I eventually came to understand the basic functions of Ruby. The goal is to reach a level of comfort with the basics of Ruby where you can have a natural conversation with someone on the topic. Although something may make sense in your end, it may not make sense to someone else when you try to say it out loud.

The following guide is not broken into particular sections. It glides through the basic concepts of Ruby I learned online. The guide will require several steps of you for it to become an effective 'cheat sheet':

  • Read the question stem.
  • Hide the answer provided.
  • Write out your own answer or say it out loud.
  • Review the answer given and compare it to your own.
  • Go back through the sheet while only looking at the question stems. Begin changing particular details such as integers or inputs to make sure you understand the concepts.
  • Go back over the questions stems over and over again to the point where you can turn it into one big conversation about Ruby in your head.
  • Remember, the point is not to memorize. The point is to get comfortable.
