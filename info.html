Joe Plonsker | {🌐}

Joe Plonsker is an amateur photographer from San Francisco, CA. He attended St. John's College in Santa Fe, NM, where he earned his BA in Philosophy and Mathematics. He has been featured in Hamburger Eyes Photography Magazine, and was also a finalist in the 2018 International Street Photography Competition.

Joe currently works as a software engineer while also teaching web development at under-resourced high schools through Code Nation.
